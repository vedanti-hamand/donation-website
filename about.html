THE SPARKS FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization, that works for providing education and food to underprevileged kids. Even in 2021, the world still continues to grapple with the COVID-19 pandemic. Our goal is that no child or young adult, especially girls, migrant and tribal children and those in street situations are deprived of education, food and the basic necessities.
